<h>91. TOÑANE YÉSUS</h>
<br> </br>
<t>Tune : Come to the Saviour. Basa 26 - Mb 93 - Bafia 195 - SAF 465</t>
<v>1</v>
		Tôñane Yésus, te beta ben,
		Emien A jô na, "Ma Me ne zen";
		Nnye fe a tele vôm bôt be too,
		A jô na, "tame zu".
<br> </br>
<c>Avak, avak, d'aye fo'o bo,</c>
<c>Eyoñ bôt b'asulan vôm A too,</c>
<c>Mbamba minlem A ve bô bese,</c>
<c>Amu b'abuni Nye.</c>
<v>2</v>
		Yeba'ane bongô be zu' be Nye
		Te kame be be za ke awu;
		Lita'ane be zen j'ake be Nye,
		A dutu be be zu.
<v>3</v>
		Simsa'ane na A ngenan A too,
		Te beta bem, te jô na, Momo;
		Ve zu be Yésus eyoñe ji,
		A ve nye nlem ôse.
<br> </br>
<a>Rev. Charles Warner Mac Cleary</a>
