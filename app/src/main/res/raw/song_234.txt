<h>234. KUTE' MA MVEBE WÔE</h>
<t>Tune : Breathe on me, breath of God. Bfia 145</t>
<v>1</v>
		Kute' ma mvebe wôé
		Mvebe y'ényiñ, A Tat
		Jalé'é ma nlem, m'akômbô bo
		Ve mam w'aliti ma
<v>2</v>
		Kute' ma mvebe wôé
		Mvebe y'ényiñ, A Tat
		Me ve wo nlô, a nlem, a nyô
		Me tôñe zene jôé
<v>3</v>
		Kute ma mvebe wôé
		Mvebe y'ényiñ, A Tat
		Me bo te wu, me vee be wo
		Enyiñ ya nnôm éto
