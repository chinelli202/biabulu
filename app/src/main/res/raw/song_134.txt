<h>134. YÉSUS KRIST A NTELE VA</h>
<br> </br>
<t>Tune : Alberson. Bafia 122</t>
<v>1</v>
		Yésus Krist A ntele va,
		Fombô'ô Nye, wo, mbo abé;
		Mbamba môs a soya den,
		Bete'e tyiñ, a loene nye.
<v>2</v>
		Tame yen A loene wo,
		"Za' avô, a mone wom".
		Kôlô'ô si a ve Nye nlem,
		Kôlô'ô si, A loene wo.
<v>3</v>
		Ko m'éngôngo, A Mba Tat,
		Liti'i me nya nye'ane Wôé;
		Nye'an ôte ô za'a me nlem,
		Nalé m'aye yen ényiñ.
<v>4</v>
		Yésus Krist A bili ngu,
		A ye saé môt a ye zu;
		A ye jô na, "kele mvo'é,
		Mbunan nwô ô nyiiya wo".
<br> </br>
<a>Rev. Mac Neill</a>
