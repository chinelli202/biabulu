<h>100. TE ME'ETE NA, BENYA MEJÔÔ</h>
<br> </br>
<t>Tune : Why not to-night. Basa 257 - Bafia 172</t>
<v>1</v>
		Te me'ete na, benya mejôô,
		Me tulane melo a ke,
		A môt, w'awôk, te bo ayet,
		W'akômbô nyiñ, w'ayange jé?
<br> </br>
<c>W'ayange jé ? w'ayange jé ?</c>
<c>W'akômbô nyiñ, w'ayange jé ?</c>
<c>W'ayange jé ? W'ayange jé ?</c>
<c>Nge w'aye nyiñ, w'ayange jé ?</c>
<v>2</v>
		Te beta yange môse mfe,
		Amu wo te yem môs Y'awu.
		Ve bo mefek a buni den,
		W'akômbô nyiñ, w'ayange jé ?
<v>3</v>
		Satan a kpwe'ele bô bese.
		A ne te yeme volô nlem,
		Za'a wulu zen é ne zôsô,
		W'akômbô nyiñ, wayange jé ?
<v>4</v>
		Yésus a ne te bene môt,
		Nge aye zu a buni nye,
		Te beta ben, buni'i ve den;
		W'akômbô nyiñ, w'ayange jé ?
