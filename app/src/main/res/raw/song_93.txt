<h>93. TATE ZAMBE, BA'ALE' BIA BESE</h>
<br> </br>
<t>Tune : God be with you. Basa 193 - Mb 246 - Bafia 202 - SAF 456</t>
<v>1</v>
		Tate Zambe, ba'ale' bia bese,
		Ba'ale' bia melu mesese'e;
		Vôm ase bi ake, nge tabe;
		Tate Zambe, ba'ale' bia bese.
<br> </br>
<c>Akekui bi ayenan</c>
<c>Bôt a Yésus nlame ya yôp,</c>
<c>Akekui bi atôban,</c>
<c>Zambe, ba'ale' bia melu mese.</c>
<v>2</v>
		Tate Zambe, ba'ale' bia bese,
		Ngule jangan é ne momo,
		Satan a za dañe bia,
		Tate Zambe ba'ale' bia bese.
<v>3</v>
		Tate Zambe, ba'ale' bia bese,
		Bi za dane yeñ ékpwe'ele,
		Ô n'ében j'akâme bia,
		Tate Zambe, ba'ale' bia bese.
<v>4</v>
		Tate Zambe ba'ale' bia bese,
		Môs awu d'azu be bia,
		Woñ ô maneya fo'o, akéva !
		Tate Zambe, ba'ale' bia bese.
<br> </br>
<a>Rev. William Moore Dager</a>
