<h>203. NYII WOM A VEE</h>
<br> </br>
<t>Tune : Tis easter time, Glad easter time</t>
<v>1</v>
		Nyii wom A vee, Nyii wom A vee.
		Yésus Nyii wom A vee;
		Zaka'an, mi beta kate na,
		Yésus Nyii wom A vee.
<br> </br>
<c>Mba môs y'avak, mba môs y'avak,</c>
<c>Amu Nyii wom A vee;</c>
<c>A wômôya soñ, A bo Nyii wom.</c>
<c>A ve m'ava'a nlem.</c>
<v>2</v>
		A loene bongô na, be zu,
		B'anye'e be toñe Nye.
		Akôsabo ayôme bon,
		E ne fe ngul ya zu.
<v>3</v>
		Nyii wongan A nga wômô son,
		Awu te dañe Nye;
		Ajô' te A ye nyii fe bôt,
		Nge b'aye buni fo'o.
<br> </br>
<a>Mrs A.R. Wolfe</a>
